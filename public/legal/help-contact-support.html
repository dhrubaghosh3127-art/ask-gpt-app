ASK-GPT Support

If you need help with app usage, API key setup, voice input, image analysis, or other technical issues, you can contact ASK-GPT Support by email.

askgpt.support@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Email

  • Your device name or phone model
  • A short and clear description of the issue
  • What you were trying to do when the issue happened
  • A screenshot, if available
  • Your app version, if relevant

Before Sending a Support Email

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Make sure your API key was entered correctly, if your issue is related to setup.
  • Try again once before sending a report.
  • Include enough detail so the issue can be understood clearly.
Important

Clear and complete details help ASK-GPT Support understand your issue faster and respond more effectively.
