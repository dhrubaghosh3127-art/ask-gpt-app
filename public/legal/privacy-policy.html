Privacy policy

ASK-GPT respects user privacy and is committed to handling user information responsibly and transparently. This Privacy Policy explains what information ASK-GPT may process, how that information may be used, how it may be stored, and how user privacy is respected when you use the app.

By accessing or using ASK-GPT, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. About ASK-GPT

ASK-GPT is an AI-powered application that may provide chat assistance, writing assistance, reasoning support, search-related features, voice-related features, and other AI-assisted tools. ASK-GPT does not operate its own standalone AI model platform. To provide app functionality, ASK-GPT may rely on third-party services, APIs, cloud infrastructure, and storage or authentication services.

2. Information We May Process

If you sign in with Google, ASK-GPT may process your email address for sign-in, account identification, account-related notices, and basic account support.

If you sign in and use saved chat features, your saved messages may be stored in Firebase so that your chats are not lost and can remain available in your account.

If you voluntarily send a report, bug report, or support request, ASK-GPT may process the contents of the report, your email address attached to the report, and any text, screenshot, image, or file you choose to submit. This information may be reviewed only for support, troubleshooting, and reply purposes.

ASK-GPT supports user-provided official Groq API keys only, including official Groq free keys and official Groq paid keys. Unless support is officially added in the future, ASK-GPT does not state support for other user-provided API providers.

According to the app’s intended design, user-provided API keys are stored locally on the user’s device and are not intended to be kept as ordinary stored records on ASK-GPT servers for normal app use. ASK-GPT is not designed as a service for routine manual reading or browsing of users’ locally stored personal API keys.

3. How We Use Information

ASK-GPT may use processed information only for the following purposes:

  • to allow user sign-in,
  • to keep saved chats available,
  • to provide app features,
  • to respond to support requests and reports,
  • to maintain app reliability and security,
  • to improve app stability and fix issues.

4. Third-Party Services

ASK-GPT may use third-party services and APIs to provide app functionality. Depending on enabled features, this may include Groq, Firebase, Gemini, and other third-party services that may be added in the future if necessary.

When ASK-GPT relies on a third-party provider to deliver a requested feature, the content necessary for that feature may be processed through that provider.

5. App Limits and API Key Usage

ASK-GPT may apply its own app-level limits, access rules, message limits, fairness controls, feature restrictions, and internal usage policies.

Using your own official Groq API key inside ASK-GPT does not automatically mean that you will receive the full provider-side quota, full provider-side capacity, or unrestricted provider-level usage inside the ASK-GPT platform. The usage available inside ASK-GPT may differ from the usage available directly from Groq outside the app. ASK-GPT may define and enforce separate app-level limits regardless of whether the user adds an official Groq free key or an official Groq paid key.

6. Data We Do Not Routinely Read

ASK-GPT is not designed as a service for routine human reading of users’ private saved chats or personal content.

However, if a user voluntarily submits a report, bug report, or support request, the contents of that submission may be reviewed only to investigate the issue and respond.

7. Data Sharing

ASK-GPT does not sell user data.

ASK-GPT may share or transmit limited information only when reasonably necessary for app operation, including with authentication or storage services such as Firebase, with AI or API providers needed to complete requested features, with service infrastructure required for app functionality, or where required for legal compliance, security, abuse prevention, or service protection.

8. Data Storage

Depending on the feature used, some data may remain on the user’s device, saved chats may be stored in Firebase if the user signs in and uses saved chat functionality, and report or support submission data may be available to us when voluntarily submitted by the user.

9. Data Security

ASK-GPT takes reasonable measures intended to help protect stored information and reduce unnecessary exposure of user data.

However, no method of storage, transmission, or online processing can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure in every situation.

10. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

If ASK-GPT provides account-based functionality, users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the in-app delete option, if available, and through the official web deletion page provided by ASK-GPT.
