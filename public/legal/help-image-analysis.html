Image Upload and Analysis

Learn how image upload works in ASK-GPT and what kind of visible content can be analyzed.

Using Image Upload

ASK-GPT supports image upload for image-based assistance and visible content analysis.

You can upload screenshots, notes, text images, documents, and other visible image content when you want the app to understand what is shown in the image.

What ASK-GPT Can Analyze

  • Visible text in screenshots or notes
  • Basic visible content shown inside an uploaded image
  • Questions related to what appears in the image
  • Image-based context that helps explain or answer your request

Before Uploading an Image

  • Make sure the image is clear and readable.
  • Use a screenshot or photo with enough visible detail.
  • Keep the important content inside the frame.
  • Try again with a clearer image if the result is incomplete.
Important

ASK-GPT supports image understanding and image analysis only. It does not support AI image generation or photo creation.

Screenshot, note, text image, document-er chobi, ba onnano visible image content upload kore apni app-ke dekhano jinis bujhate parben.

ASK-GPT ki ki analyze korte pare

  • Screenshot ba note-er vitore visible text
  • Upload kora image-er vitore dekhte paoa basic visible content
  • Image-e ja dekhano ache, tar upor vitti kore kora prosno
  • Image-based context ja answer dite help kore

Image upload korar age

  • Image jeno clear ar readable hoy.
  • Screenshot ba chobi-te jeno jothesto visible detail thake.
  • Important content frame-er moddhe rakho.
  • Result incomplete hole aro clear image diye abar try koro.
